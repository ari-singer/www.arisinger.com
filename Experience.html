Experience

Amazon

As a Software Development Engineer Intern at Amazon, I had the oppportunity to develop an internal service that is being used within the Digital Ads Branch on a day to day basis. I worked with many technologies that were brand new to me, such as Java, TypeScript, and many AWS technologies including Lambda, DynamoDB, S3, ECS, CDK, IAM. I grew within my communication and teamwork competencies in this experience, as I had to present my service to the greater organization at the conclusion of my internship, as well as work within an agile development team.

ElementaryConnect

Towards the end of the summer of 2020, my friend and I co-founded a company in which local young families can be paired with mentors to supervise their children during the virtual school day, providing our local community with a much-needed service. In creating our LLC, I learned and executed on many aspects of creating a business, including formulating a business plan, filing as a registered entity, initial advertising, and initial launch. I grew within my entrepreneurial mindset as well as persistence competencies in launching this company, as it took a lot of hard work to get it off the ground.

Microelectronics RL Team

I was an apprentice researcher on Professor Jay Guo's Multidisciplinary Design Program's reasearch team that focused on Machine Learning techniques. My subteam, the microelectronics reinforcement learning team used offline RL techniques to learn optimal printing parameters for inkjet printers. The application of this is to allow printers to print curcuits directly onto paper for quick R and D purposes. We worked with a team based in Boise State University to receive printing data and images through experimentation and used this data to train the model itself.

MFAMS

Gamer Saloon is an online platform where professional (and recreational) gamers can wager real money on an online match in any of their favorite games. In interning at Gamer Saloon, I created a plug-in using PHP for the Gamer Saloon blog to allow for the automatic refresh of upcoming matches by pulling from their AWS database. I also gleaned beta testing experience by testing their new app in beta. I grew within my creativity competency in this experience as it took many different approaches to try and find the root causes of the known bugs.

Resolute BI

Resolute Building Intelligence is a Michigan based technology company that works towards helping buisinesses optimize energy use within their building operations. Their platform uses automatic and monitored HVAC systems with a data driven control platform that helps administrators make choices to minimize energy consumption (and thus cost) while keeping the internal climate maintained for the people in it. I grew within my communication competency as I was working with engineers across the globe in testing out new prototype hardware for the company.
